PDXaTUG February: Mapping with Tableau!

Our presenters: Brittany Brannon of Slalom Consulting and PK Kase of Oregon Department of Transportation. Brit and PK will lead us in a hands-on knowledge-share of their work with Tableau mapping.

This was a joint meeting with the MaptimePDX Meetup group.


What to bring: Bring your laptop with Tableau. If you don’t have Tableau, download a trial. You will also want to have a Mapbox account.
